AGMR, SLAM, Sensor fusion, Kalman Filter, AMCL, BLDC motor, Web server, ROS2, C++, Python, Vue.js

Multi-purpose Autonomous Mobile robot

Private company - Busan, South Korea

2023~2024

limited information due to non-disclosure agreement


Update of previous robotic platform with the implementation of embedded web server, battery level monitoring, LEDs signage, extra proximity sensors and more reliable navigation.

I was still the team leader and the designer of the project. I worked on the navigation part, mechanical part and build of the robot as well as the Webserver Front end part and helping for back end part.
My 2 developers worked on updating the ROS part and improved the reliability of the robot and webserver as well as helping 3 interns on the computer vision part.



Keywords:
C++, Python, ROS2, Vuejs, LiDAR SLAM, Visual SLAM, 3D design, Solidworks, R.Pi5, R.Pi Zero, Arduino Nano, RGB camera, Project manager, Notion

Mechanical points

I kept the aluminum profile frame structure and created a second robot frame with some advancements.
The previous frame design was updated and I built a new frame with larger base to include the wheels on the chassis and corner space for proximity sensors.
I designed and 3D printed handlers for proximity sensors, RGB-D camera handler mounted on servo motor and power box.
I also designed steel hub motors handlers and requested to a workshop for their fabrication.
